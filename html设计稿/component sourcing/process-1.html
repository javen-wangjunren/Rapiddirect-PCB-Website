Our Sourcing Process

A transparent and controlled workflow ensuring original parts and on-time delivery.

01. Submit BOM

Upload your Bill of Materials (BOM) in Excel or CSV format. Our system and engineers instantly analyze your part numbers for availability.

[ Real Photo: A clear engineering BOM file displayed on a high-res tablet screen ]

02. BOM Review & RFQ

Our senior procurement engineers review every line item, verifying MPNs and suggested quantities to provide a precise Request for Quote.

[ Real Photo: A senior procurement engineer reviewing technical specs on dual monitors ]

03. Supplier Matching

We leverage our global network to match your needs with authorized manufacturers and Tier-1 distributors, ensuring 100% original sourcing.

[ Real Photo: Global supply chain map icons paired with authorized distributor logos ]

04. Price & Lead Time

Get a comprehensive report on real-time pricing and confirmed lead times. We offer cost-down alternatives for long-lead items for your approval.

[ Real Photo: A formal quotation report showing transparent price breakdowns and dates ]

05. Order & Procurement

Upon your confirmation, we execute the purchase immediately. Our ERP system tracks every reel and tray until it reaches our facility.

[ Real Photo: Professional purchasing staff executing orders in an ERP management system ]

06. IQC Incoming Inspection

Crucial stage: 100% inspection including X-ray anti-counterfeit testing, visual verification, and quantity counts in our ESD-safe labs.

[ Real Photo: IQC inspector using a digital microscope to check component markings ]

07. Delivery / PCBA

Parts are either vacuum-packed for worldwide shipment or seamlessly transferred to our internal SMT lines for assembly.

[ Real Photo: Vacuum-sealed component reels with professional traceability labels ]
