Closed-Loop Quality Control

A rigorous 4-stage quality management system tailored for high-precision PCB manufacturing and PCBA assembly, ensuring zero defects from raw materials to final delivery.

IQC
Incoming Control
Strict admission control for substrates, components, and chemicals. Preventing defective materials at the source.
IPQC & FAI
In-Process & First Article
Mandatory First Article Inspection (FAI) combined with scheduled line patrolling for zero-deviation mass production.
FQC
Final Quality Control
Comprehensive visual and electrical validation to ensure every finished board meets IPC standards.
QA / OQC
Outgoing Assurance
Final verification of packaging, barcode traceability, and pre-shipment sampling to close the quality loop.
