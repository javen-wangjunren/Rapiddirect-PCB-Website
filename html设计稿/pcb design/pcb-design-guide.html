Annular Ring
≥ 4 mil (recommended) for stable plating and better yield.
Solder Mask Expansion
4–6 mil typical. Adjust for fine pitch to prevent solder bridging.
Copper Balance
Balance copper across layers to reduce bow/twist and stabilize impedance.
Edge Clearance
Keep copper/vias ≥ 0.5 mm from the outline; increase for V-cut and high voltage.
Controlled Impedance
Lock stackup, targets, tolerance, and coupons before routing critical nets.
Design Advice
  • High-speed routing: plan return paths and reference planes early; keep stubs short and rules consistent.
  • DFM-first footprints: watch solder mask slivers, and verify paste apertures for QFN/LGA thermal pads.
  • Testability: reserve accessible test points for rails and key signals, away from tall components.
